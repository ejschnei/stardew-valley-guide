Fish Guide Legend & Tips:

Blue rows: Community Center Bundle fish

Orange rows: Legendary fish (can only be caught once each)

Extended Family fish: Special legendary variants only available during Mr. Qi's Extended Family quest (3-day time limit)

Fishing Tips:

  • Higher fishing level = easier to catch fish and access to better tackle
  • Rain affects several fish availability - check weather conditions
  • Use bait to make fish bite faster, tackle for easier catching
  • Crab pots are great for passive income and bundle completion
  • Some fish only appear in specific areas of locations (like East Pier for Crimsonfish)
  • Legendary fish are one-time catches - don't sell them until you've completed the bundle!
